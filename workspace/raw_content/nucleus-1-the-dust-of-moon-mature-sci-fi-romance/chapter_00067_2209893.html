Hello, readers!

As Nucleus approaches its 50th chapter, I've been analyzing how to enhance your reading experience while working Royal Road's ecosystem. After some research (and a bit of algorithm sleuthing...it's what I do, yeah?), I've decided to try something new with longer chapters.

Starting with Chapter 50, when a chapter exceeds 4,000 words, I'll be splitting it into two scenes with a two-hour gap between releases. This means:

  • Scene 1 will arrive at the usual time (5pm CEST / 11am ET / 8am PT)
  • Scene 2 will follow approximately 2 hours later
  • Both scenes will still release on the same day

Why the change? It turns out this approach offers benefits for both casual and dedicated readers. The 2-hour window gives everyone enough time to digest Scene 1 without waiting until next week for the conclusion. Meanwhile, it helps Nucleus appear twice on Royal Road's Latest Updates list, potentially introducing this series to new readers.
