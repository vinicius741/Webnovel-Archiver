Discord Emoji Preview 1

Hello, readers,

The characters of Nucleus face incredible challenges in a universe filled with danger, but what keeps them going is something deeply human: connection. In our own world, Discord has become a place where many of us find those same connections, building communities around shared interests and stories.

That's why I've decided to create a Discord server dedicated to Nucleus ! And of course, no proper server is complete without custom emojis. To start, I've designed sets for the 8 key characters who will appear across multiple books/seasons of the story.

Is that a spoiler? Perhaps a tiny one! But I hope you'll enjoy this preview of what's to come...
