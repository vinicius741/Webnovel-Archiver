“What do you plan to do?” Emma asked.

Several mammoths had already passed beneath them, and they were already getting to the end of the herd.

She leapt from the tree like a cartoon figure, jumping ahead of the last mammoth. Karlack swung down incredibly far, and the whip shrank at the same time, until she was right beneath the tusk.

Karlack jumped onto the creature’s back and ran up its body towards its head. When Karlack reached the back of the mammoth’s neck, she snapped her whip around the beast’s throat.

The creature tried to blow on its horn like a trumpet to signal help, but with its throat completely choked by the whip, no sounds emerged.
