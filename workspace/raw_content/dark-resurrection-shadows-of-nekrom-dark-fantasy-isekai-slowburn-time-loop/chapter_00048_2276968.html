Its source was a river flowing from the south, where the mountain ranges with frozen peaks were located, which, in the Evil Dream, could not be seen due to the extremely dense darkness, not even with the aid of the reddish light retained in the clouds.

Its mouth lay to the west, toward a river that crossed the rest of the Domain of End-World until it found its end in the Onyx Sea.

"Ah, the sea... Take me far away, so may I never come back...," Tristessa sang inside her head, a bloody smile plastered on her sickly face.

The witch, Lorraine, roughly dropped her, causing her to slam her entire body against the countless flat, ash-covered stones that made up the lake's shoreline. For the girl, there was no more pain to feel, only a savage thirst increased by the sound of the dark water lapping over the stones, cleansing them and carrying the remains of ash into the depths.

What appeared among the swirling clouds, amidst the darkness of the center of the vortex, were massive black gears. Dozens of them, spinning rapidly on the surface of dark metal machinery that expelled gaseous Discord between pistons and valves that opened and closed non-stop.

The terror that gripped Tristessa was ineffable. Two monstrous women; an angel and a demoness, the first embracing the second from behind. Both corrupted by gears, Discord, and metallic parts connected to their rotten and eldritch bodies.

The Baptism in Ruins, for the first time since experiencing her first Death, ached in Tristessa's chest.
